Core Features

Everything you need on the road

01

DBC Signal Decoding

Import DBC files and automatically decode CAN messages into physical signal values. Multiple DBCs supported simultaneously.

02

Multi-Signal Chart Overlay

Overlay multiple signal waveforms on a shared time axis. Compare engine speed, vehicle speed, temperature, and more at a glance.

03

Offline-First Design

All parsing happens locally — your log data never leaves your device. Fully functional on road tests, in workshops, or anywhere without connectivity.

04

Quick Search & Filter

Jump to any timestamp, filter by CAN ID or channel, scan by signal value range. Find the data you care about in seconds.
